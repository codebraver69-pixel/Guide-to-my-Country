Lebanon is a small but historically rich country located on the eastern coast of the mediterranean see

it shares borders with syria to the north and east, palestine to the south and the mediterranean see to the west

despite its small size(10,452 square kilometers)Lebanon is known for it's stunning natural diversity,deep history and vibrant culture

The Lebanese flag features a horizontal triband with two red stripes at the top and bottom, separated by a central white stripe of double the height. In the center of the white stripe is a green cedar of Lebanon tree. The red stripes symbolize the blood shed in defense of the country, the white represents peace and snow, and the cedar symbolizes eternity, holiness, peace, and longevity.
